CAPTURING THE MOMENTS THAT MATTER

SHUTTER STORM

Shutter Storm is a multi-media company primarily based in the world of competitive dance. We specialize in the art of archiving creative and artistic movement through photography and videography. Our highly trained staff strive to ensure that the dedication, hard work, and emotion of countless hours of rehearsal are captured for a lifetime.
